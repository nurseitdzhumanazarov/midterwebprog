Admissions & How to Apply

Overview of the application steps and quick glance at tuition and fees.

Application Steps

  1. Create an account and complete the registration form.
  2. Upload transcripts and proof of identity.
  3. Pay the application fee and schedule an interview (if required).
  4. Receive decision and accept offer.
