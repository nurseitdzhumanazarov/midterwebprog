About Engineering College

We offer career-oriented engineering programs with strong industry links — practical labs, small classes, and career services.

Programs Overview

Below are our main programs grouped by level.

B.Sc. Mechanical Engineering

Focus: mechanics, dynamics, material science, and manufacturing. Includes hands-on labs and capstone design.

  • Duration: 4 years
  • Mode: Full-time / Part-time

B.Sc. Electrical & Electronics

Focus: circuits, signals, embedded systems, and power systems with strong lab practice.

B.Sc. Computer Science & Engineering

Focus: software engineering, data structures, and systems programming.

M.Eng. Robotics & Automation

Graduate program with research projects and industry collaboration.

Careers & Outcomes

Graduates work in manufacturing, energy, automation, software, and consulting. Average placement support and employer network available.
